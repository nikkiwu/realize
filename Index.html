PHINGERIN SS//2018

After its Spring/Summer 2018 collection was unveiled last year, Japanese streetwear imprint PHINGERIN has continued to work with bright colors and bold prints for its latest release. The Fall/Winter 2018 collection features a range of staple pieces such as outwear items, hoodies and graphic sweats as well as a range of hats and accessories.

Some of the key pieces in this release include patchwork takes on tailoring, lightweight mac-style coats and relaxed tartan trousers. Elsewhere, the collection features caps and berets finished with patterns such as a multi-colored lining and a green-and-white leopard print. Take a look through the full lookbook above, and keep an eye on the PHINGERIN web store for it to arrive.

IISE SS//2018

South Korean label IISE unveils a new lookbook for its Spring/Summer 2018 “Collection 006” range, which echoes the balance and symmetry of traditional Korean furniture. The seasonal collection is anchored by a clean color palette of navy, black, white and beiges, with a pop of burgundy for some rich vibrancy.

The unisex range includes outerwear silhouettes such as long coats, track jackets, bombers, panel coach jackets and tech vests featuring color-blocking and linear designs. Other staples such as bowler shirts, essential tee, track bottoms and lounge pants round out the essential collection. All pieces are made with premium linen and materials sourced from Korea.

Check out IISE’s latest “Collection 006” lookbook above, which was shot by Seoul-based photographer Jennifer Cheng, and head over to IISE’s web store to shop the pieces. For more wardrobe essentials, take a closer look at The North Face’s “Black Series” collection.
